Mountain Bikes

A mountain bike is a bicycle designed for off-road cycling. If you are planning on biking on trails, unpaved surfaces, rocks, or dirt then a mountain bike is for you.

What makes out mountain bikes so durable are its big wheels and strong brakes. We also have confort grip handle bars to make your ride as comfortable as possible.

The brands we offer:

  • Trek
  • Santa Cruz

Road Bikes

Road bikes are designed to be ridden fast on smooth pavement.They have thinner wheels and a skinnier frame to take you longer distances. Our bikes have a drop-bar handlebar and are best suited for fitness riding.

If you want to enjoy a nice day riding your bike, but are on a budget then this is the bike for you!

The brands we offer:
  • Bianchi
  • Cervelo
